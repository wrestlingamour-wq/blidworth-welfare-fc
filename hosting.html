Reality check

What most clubs at your level actually do.

Pitchero (~70% of clubs)

Free tier covers the basics; paid tiers (~£30–£50/mo) add ticketing, e-commerce, branded app. FA full-time is native. You lose custom design but gain zero dev dependency.

WordPress + volunteer admin (~20%)

Cheap hosting, generic theme, volunteer manages everything. Gets messy over time but works if you have a dedicated person. ~£120/yr all-in.

Custom bespoke (~5%)

What we've built. Usually paid for by a sponsor or a tech-minded committee member. Looks sharp, differentiates the club, needs real dev support to maintain.

Facebook only (~5%)

No website. Social posts cover announcements, FA Full-Time takes the fixture load. Free, simple, limited.

My honest take
For your centenary, the custom site is a lovely statement. But to make it sustainable without burning out a volunteer, the Tier 2 admin layer is essential. Roughly £800 one-off + £300/year, and anyone can run it. Lotto + sponsor contributions cover it easily. If budget is genuinely zero, Pitchero is the sensible answer — not this site.
